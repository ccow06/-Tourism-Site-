Welcome to the SCP Foundation official home page! We here at SCP want to provide safer experience for everyone involved, as so we would like your help! For more information with applying, click on the links on the side, or keep reading for information about our esteemed organization, which operates out of more than 100 countries worldwide!

We here at the SCP Foundation are accepting any and all forms of help. We are committed to helping to bring humanity up, and keep those that may keep it down contained, away from where they can do harm. Together, we can make a safe world, where no one needs to worry about their safety ever again!

From janitorial staff to chief researchers, we are looking for all sorts of ambitious people to help us in our quest. Whatever your skillset, your presence is humbly requested in this organization of ours! Site managers, governmental diplomats, security guards, and even chefs! We foster a warm and welcoming environment within the walls of our sites. If you are interested, click the link on the left that says "Apply here" to apply today! (Requires filing of Danger Acknowledgement Waiver, as well as the SCP Safety Contract.)
